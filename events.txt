<p>Kshitij, the annual techno-management fest of IIT Kharagpur provides a platform for a confluence of students, academia, and the industry. </p>
<br>
<br> 
<p><h4> <u>General information: </u></h4></p>
<p>While the competitions at Kshitij prove to be the ultimate test of grit, knowledge and skill, the guest lectures and workshops provide an enriching experience for the avid learners. The fest has been graced by the presence of such luminaries who are looked up to for their distinguished contribution in their respective fields. The upcoming 14th edition is going to be held from 27th-29th January 2017.

Our glorious journey has been recognized by UNESCO, UNICEF, SAYEN and CEE as they offered us their patronage and appreciated our efforts by associating with our social initiatives.

Above all, with over 70,000 participation per year and a prize money of over INR 5 million, it is the Asia'a largest Techno-Management Fest.
</p>
